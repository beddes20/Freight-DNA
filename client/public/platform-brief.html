Recently Released — Use These Now
Carrier Lane Search New
Pick an origin, destination, and radius — instantly see every carrier that has run that corridor from your own freight history. Loads hauled, market share %, avg carrier pay, last ship date. Grouped by mode (Van, Reefer, Flatbed, LTL, Drayage, IMDL). Build a call list in under 60 seconds when freight drops.
RFP Lane Search New
Search across every uploaded RFP in the platform by corridor. Instantly see which customers have freight on specific lanes, grouped by mode. Know which accounts to call before an RFP hits your inbox — proactive, not reactive.
DNA Guru Lane Intel New
Ask DNA Guru in plain English: "What carriers run TX to CA for dry vans?" or "How much are we paying CA-TX?" — get a live answer from your actual freight data. No navigation, no filters. Just ask.
Procurement Prompt New
When you save a new award that has high-volume lanes (50+ loads/year), the platform now immediately asks if you want to create procurement tasks for your team. One click to launch the sourcing workspace — no award gets dropped after the win.
Where We're Leaving Value on the Table
🔗
Contact Lane Attributions
Highest Impact Gap
If contacts aren't tied to freight corridors, the Relationship Freight portlets show nothing useful. The 1st/2nd/3rd Base system only generates signal when lanes are actually assigned to contacts.
→ Assign lanes to your top 10 contacts this week
📞
Pre-Call Planner
60 Seconds That Change the Call
Synthesizes AI health narrative, last 5 touchpoint notes, lane gap talking points, and relationship intel before you dial. Most reps skip it. Those who use it have better calls.
→ Open it before every new prospect call
🤖
DNA Guru Actions
It's More Than Search
DNA Guru can log touchpoints and create tasks through chat — not just answer questions. Say "Log a call with Jane at Acme, she confirmed the Q3 bid" and it creates the touchpoint with one confirm tap.
→ Try logging your next call through Guru
📈
Relationship Base Advancement
The "Down, Not Across" Engine
If contacts are sitting at 1st Base indefinitely, there's no progression data to coach from. Every meaningful conversation is an opportunity to advance a contact. The history timeline tracks every move.
→ Review base levels on your top 5 accounts
💬
Meaningful Conversation Flag
Data Quality Issue
Without checking "meaningful conversation" on real touchpoints, the platform can't distinguish 10 voicemails from 10 real conversations. Cold contact alerts and health scores depend on this flag being accurate.
→ Check the box on real conversations, not every log
💰
Wallet Share in Conversations
The Number That Closes Deals
The platform calculates wallet share from financial uploads — but are reps referencing it? Knowing an account does $2M in freight and you're touching $300K is a conversation starter and a coaching lever.
→ Pull wallet share before every account review
🎯
Goals & 1:1 Sessions
Management Layer
Are all NAMs setting live goals for all their AMs? Are 1:1 sessions being closed with summaries and morale scores? Auto-tracking only works when targets exist and sessions are completed properly.
→ Audit open goals and close any pending sessions
📋
Account Intelligence Fields
Institutional Knowledge at Risk
Portal credentials, tendering processes, account quirks, and operating hours live in the platform — but only if someone fills them in. Empty fields mean every rep re-discovers the same information from scratch.
→ Fill in your top 20 accounts this quarter
📊
Daily Digest & Trends Feed
Habits Drive the ROI
The daily digest delivers AI-prioritized tasks, cold contacts, and RFP deadlines each morning. The Callouts & Trends feed is only valuable if the team posts to it. Both require consistent habits to deliver value.
→ One post to Trends per week, per rep minimum
Platform philosophy: "Down, not across" — unlock wallet share in accounts you already own before chasing new logos.

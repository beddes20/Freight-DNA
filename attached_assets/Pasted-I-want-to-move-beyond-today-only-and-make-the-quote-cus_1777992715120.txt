Part 2 — Customer ownership model

I want a permanent ownership model so customer/distribution inboxes don’t create assignment ambiguity.

Please add or formalize this in the customer profile:

Customer owner rep

Every customer should have a single primary owner rep/user.

This owner is the default fallback for quote assignment when an inbound email clearly belongs to that customer but does not match a specific contact.

Customer email identities

Support multiple email identities on the customer:

primary domain(s),

named contact emails,

shared/distribution emails (examples: quotes@, shipping@, traffic@, logistics@, etc.).

These should be stored as explicit customer-linked email identities, not left to fuzzy matching only.

Assignment precedence
When an inbound quote-related email is matched to a customer, assign the rep in this order:

exact contact email match with known owner if one exists,

exact customer-linked shared/distribution email match,

known customer domain match,

customer owner rep fallback,

Needs Routing only if still ambiguous.

UI
In the customer profile, add fields/sections for:

Owner rep

Allowed customer domains

Shared/distribution customer emails

Key contact emails

Quote Requests UI
Once a quote is linked to a customer, the Rep column should show:

the explicit matched owner when known,

otherwise the customer owner fallback,

and only show a blank/unknown state if the customer itself truly has no owner configured.

Here's a rundown of where email shows up across the platform. It's actually one of the most pervasive integrations in Freight DNA — it touches outreach, intake, notifications, auth, and analytics.

1. Core email plumbing
Outbound dispatcher — central service that sends mail via Resend or SMTP, plus shared HTML templates.
Microsoft 365 / Graph integration — used for tracked outreach (creates a draft, then sends, so it can capture message and conversation IDs and inject a central reply-to).
Shared inbox webhooks — Graph change-notification subscriptions that monitor a central mailbox plus enrolled user mailboxes for incoming replies.
2. Carrier outreach & procurement
Lane Work Queue / proactive outreach — automated and manual carrier emails, with reply tracking (Quote Received, Pass, No Response).
Carrier intel enrichment — parses inbound carrier emails to update contacts, capacity, and lane preferences in the CRM.
Procurement workspace UI — the carrier outreach panel where reps draft and send.
3. Customer quoting & market intel
Quote email ingestion — auto-captures inbound RFQs from customer emails into Freight Capture.
Spot quote intake — routes inbound spot requests into the Freight Triage workflow.
Win/loss classifiers — AI reads email threads to mark quotes won or lost.
Email response-time analytics — measures rep responsiveness and correlates it with win rates.
4. Automated notifications & digests
Daily morning briefing — AI-summarized priorities, overdue tasks, and churn-risk alerts.
Rep / manager performance reports — weekly and monthly progress and goal updates.
1:1 coaching recaps — meeting summaries after NAM/AM sessions.
Feedback & bug routing — user-submitted feedback emailed to the dev team.
5. Auth & system admin
Password reset — standard self-service recovery flow.
Unprovisioned sign-in alerts — notifies admins when a Clerk sign-in lacks a local profile.
Integration health alerts — admin notifications when Graph or SMTP providers are degraded.
6. User-facing UI
Conversations module — a shared-inbox view inside Freight DNA for reading, snoozing, and replying to email threads.
Draft email modal & outreach templates — UI for managing templates and previewing AI-generated drafts.
Admin monitored-mailboxes page — where admins enroll M365 mailboxes for ingestion.

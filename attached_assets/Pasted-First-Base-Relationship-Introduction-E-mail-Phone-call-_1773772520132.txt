First Base Relationship: 

Introduction 

E-mail/Phone call (still feels uncomfortable)  

We provide options and solutions quickly from the first opportunity 

Find a way to get freight, it could be a spot load we lose money on, but we have to build trust and relevance.  

This is typically a very transactional relationship at this point. 

Money typically goes as follows (somewhat dependent on where this person is in the org chart): 

$........(days go by without moving freight)........$.......................$...........$ 

 

Second Base Relationship: 

We’ve built trust, but still have a newer relationship.  

Memes/Gifs in e-mail, possibly teams messaging/texting 

You get to know their interests outside of work 

You know what makes them look good to their boss 

Freight is still transactional. Maybe some opportunity to transition spot freight to contracted freight.  

They might introduce you to members of their team who need help. 

Money typically goes as follows: 

$......$........$..........$..........$ 

 

Third Base Relationship: 

You’re a trusted carrier. You’ve bailed them out more than once. 

You are texting/calling from your cell phone. You have met them in person, or have plans to meet them in person.  

You have the opportunity to takeover lanes they are struggling with (service or money) 

You are starting to become an extension of their supply chain. 

You will start getting transparency into projects, chunks of freight, and freight that is not on the RFP. 

Freight is NOT just transactional. It turns to contractual with CHUNKS of freight.  

Price starts to become secondary. 

They give you warm introductions to other load planners, even if they don’t have huge needs. 

Money typically goes as follows: 

$$$$....$$....$$$$$....$$$$$.....$$$$$....$$ 

 

 

 

Homerun Relationship: 

This person has become a friend. (more fulfilling than you might think) 

You are absolutely an extension of their supply chain. They will call you for market updates, eyes and ears in the market, etc. 

You get first access to projects, newly acquired lanes, etc 

You will have feedback/communication in RFP’s that only a handful of carriers might get.  

You’re talked about in a very positive light in their team meetings. Hot introductions.  

Price becomes secondary. We are more than just a rate. Trust is proven. 

You’re dealing with projects, surges, CHUNKS of freight. 

Money typically goes as follows: 

$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$

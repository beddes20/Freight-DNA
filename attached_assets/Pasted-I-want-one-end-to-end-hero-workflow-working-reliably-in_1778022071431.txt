Customer email → Quote captured & replied → Quote marked Won → Load ready in Available Freight for the right LM.
This must be wired using the existing four surfaces:
•	Conversations / email
•	Customer Quotes / Quote Requests
•	Available Freight
•	Lane Work Queue[Memory]

2.	Implement the missing glue and corrections.
3.	Prove the full loop works in production with a real specimen.[Memory]
Target hero loop (v1)
For the chosen segment, the system should support:
1.	A customer sends a quote request email.
2.	That email is ingested and appears in Conversations and/or the Quote Requests view.
3.	A quote record is created/updated in Customer Quotes with:
•	customer attached
•	lane (origin/destination)
•	requested date/time
•	owning rep
1.	The rep replies / prices the quote.
2.	When the quote is marked Won:
•	create or update a corresponding load / freight record in Available Freight with lane, customer, dates, and rep.
•	make that record visible in the appropriate LM’s Lane Work Queue in a ready-to-book state.[Memory]
